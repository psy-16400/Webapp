WELCOME TO KALESHWARNATH PRATISHTHAN

We are family a of around 150 volunteers engaged in social works to keep marathi community & literature alive. We unite as one under the name, a roof "Kaleshwarnath". We carry out parades, perform arts, into music, run a troup called "Kaleshwarnath Dhol Pathak", a one of the most beloved troup in mumbai having three branches starting from Pune, Mumbai and at Surat, Gujrat spreading marathi culter beyond our state. We encourage you all to be a helping in this too, enjoy arts. Register and enroll for admissions. Do check out through videos and posts, look out for upcoming events.
